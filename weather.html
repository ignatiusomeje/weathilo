Welcome to Weathilo App

This App helps farmers to know the weather condition of their area and the type of crops that they can plant in that area and season

The App uses your geolocation to location you and if it is not active, it uses your IP address to track your location and then uses uses a weather forecast API to get the weather report of that area and uses Gemini Ai to tell the posibility of flood happening and also the type of crops that you can plant there
